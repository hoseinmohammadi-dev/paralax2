TIPS &

01

PICK A TOOL
AND STICK TO IT

Every image generator has its own visual style, so using the same prompt across different tools can produce completely different results. This variability makes it hard to iterate quickly or maintain consistency across a project. The fastest way to get reliable, usable outputs is to pick one tool and learn how it behaves. Once you're familiar with its quirks, you’ll spend less time troubleshooting and more time creating.

02

03

EVERY WORD
MATTERS

When crafting prompts, brevity is key. The more words you add, the more diluted your intent becomes. Most tools have token limits anyway so focus on the words that directly affect composition, subject, or style, and cut the rest. Clear, intentional prompts lead to faster and more consistent results.

04

USE CALLBACKS

If you're working with multiple characters or recurring elements, assign a unique name or label to each one. AI models can easily lose track of who’s who without consistent reference points. Assign a name or tag to each character and reuse it every time they appear in your prompt to reduce visual drift. It might feel overly structured, but it’s the best way to keep things visually consistent across images.

humanize

WHY IT
WORKS

01

BRING YOUR
BRAND TO LIFE

A character gives your brand a personality—something people can relate to emotionally. This makes your brand feel more approachable and engaging. Instead of interacting with a logo or wordmark, users engage with a “character” that feels alive, expressive, and trustworthy. For AI products (like ours), where the underlying systems can often feel cold or complex, a mascot makes the product feel friendly and empathetic—more like a helpful guide than a tool.

02

BOOST RECALL
& RECOGNITION

People are wired to remember faces and stories more than static symbols. A mascot acts like a visual anchor, reinforcing memory through consistent presence and personality. Studies show that branded characters can significantly increase recall and recognition because they give the brain something to associate and replay. If you have a unique, expressive mascot (like a cute anthropomorphic flame), users will instantly recognize and remember your brand in a sea of lookalike logos.

03

SAY MORE
WITH LESS

A mascot can make abstract product qualities like speed, ease of use, or scalability tangible and visual. It’s much easier to show that your product is “fast” by portraying your character zipping through space, than by saying “fast” in a headline. Characters can embody your product’s value props in ways that are immediate, emotional, and universally understood, no extra copy or explanation required. Whether you want to show intelligence, agility, strength, or simplicity, your mascot can become a living metaphor for those attributes, turning your product’s benefits into a visual language users instantly grasp.

04

UNLIMITED
POSSIBILITIES

Mascots are incredibly flexible tools for storytelling. They can appear in different contexts, adapt to various campaigns, and express a range of emotions or scenarios without needing to rewrite your brand language. Beyond just being visual symbols, characters can embody your users’ struggles, goals, and moments of success. By showing the mascot face challenges like debugging, scaling infrastructure, or celebrating a successful model deployment, you make the user feel seen and understood. This creates empathy and connection while allowing your brand to explain complex ideas in a more human, relatable way.
